What to Expect

🎓 What I Offer

  • Regular one-to-one supervision meetings (weekly or fortnightly)
  • Technical mentorship in DNN introspection, autonomous systems, and AI safety
  • Access to GPU compute resources and real automotive datasets
  • Guidance on publications and conference attendance
  • Network access via EU projects (EVENTS, Hi-Drive) and QUB collaborators
  • Constructive, timely feedback on writing and research ideas

💡 What I Look For

  • Background in machine learning, computer vision, or signal processing
  • Python proficiency (PyTorch or TensorFlow experience preferred)
  • Curiosity and willingness to engage deeply with literature
  • Self-motivation and ability to manage your own research timeline
  • Good written and spoken English
  • Interest in safety-critical applications — autonomous vehicles, robotics, or health

I am always looking for motivated PhD students. If you are interested in my research areas, please reach out via email to discuss potential opportunities.
