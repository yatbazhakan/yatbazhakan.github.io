excerpt: 'This study explores the effects of activity transitions on the STA-based activity recognition approach. It investigates how the accuracy of the STA-based approach is affected when activity transitions with lower probabilities are considered, and when a different approach is used to compute transition probabilities. The experiments with the dataset previously used for the evaluation of the approach does not reveal considerable differences in the accuracy.'
