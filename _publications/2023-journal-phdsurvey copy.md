# permalink: /publication/2023-journal-phdsurvey
excerpt: 'In this comprehensive review, a thorough examination is conducted on the techniques for introspection of perception errors in Automated Driving Systems (ADS). The paper categorises existing methods based on their focus areas such as object detection, classification, and semantic segmentation. It highlights the vulnerabilities in deep neural network-based perception systems that can result in safety-critical failures. The review discusses the pros and cons of current introspection techniques and identifies significant gaps in the existing literature. The study serves as a roadmap for future research aimed at enhancing the reliability and trustworthiness of perception systems in ADS.'
