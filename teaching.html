Approach

Teaching Philosophy

I connect theory to real-world engineering challenges, encouraging students to think critically about the safety and societal implications of the systems they build.

🔗

Theory Meets Practice

Concepts are anchored in real autonomous systems — from LiDAR pipelines to DNN integrity monitoring in deployed vehicles.

🛡️

Safety-First Thinking

Students reason about failure modes and safety constraints from day one, treating them as core engineering concerns.

🧪

Hands-On Learning

Lab sessions use real datasets and open-source tools to build genuine engineering confidence and reproducibility skills.

💬

Open-Door Policy

Students are always welcome to discuss ideas, ask questions, or explore research directions beyond the curriculum.
